# Story

**The monsters are real, and the hunt is on.**

As an intrepid crew of cryptid hunters, you’re embarking on daring expeditions to track down beasts of legend unknown to modern science. Out in the wild, sightings are fleeting—a scaled tail vanishing into the brush or glowing eyes gleaming in the dark. 

You'll work together to draft the bizarre traits of whatever is lurking in the shadows, piecing together a shared silhouette of the creature. But when the dust settles, it's every hunter for themselves! Armed with a hand of your favorite cryptids, you must play the card that best matches the group's sighting. Prove your expertise, claim the closest match to win the trick, and cement your legacy as the ultimate monster hunter!

# Cryptid Attributes

There are 64 unique cryptids in the game, each with a distinct set of 12 attributes
(grouped into 6 categories, each with a color).

## Head (blue)

Bones on Head (icons/head-bone.png): Features rigid antlers, jagged horns, or an exposed skull face.

Fins on Head (icons/head-fin.png): Features vibrant frills, dorsal fins, or external gills on the head.

## Eyes (purple)

Beady Eyes (icons/eyes-beady.png): Sly eyes that actively glow or burn with an unnatural light.

Compound Eyes (icons/eyes-compound.png): Faceted eyes (like a mantis) or a cluster of multiple eyes (like an arachnid).

## Mouth (red)

Fangs (icons/mouth-fangs.png): A maw with oversized tusks, sabers, or rows of jagged teeth.

Beak (icons/mouth-beak.png): A snapping, rigid beak (avian, turtle-like, or cephalopod).

## Hands (green)

Webbed Pads (icons/hands-webbed.png): Digits connected by thick aquatic webbing or bulbous suction cups.

Claws (icons/hands-claws.png): Digits ending in massive, curved talons designed for slashing.

## Skin (brown)

Fur (icons/skin-fur.png): Covered in thick fur, hair, or wool -- either solid or with stripes.

Scales (icons/skin-scales.png): Covered in scales, scutes, or overlapping bony plates.

## Tail (purple)

Spiked (icons/tail-club.png): A jagged, spiky back ridge ending in a heavy, club-like tail.

Tentacles (icons/tail-tentacles.png): A smooth or sleek back ending in tentacles or a long, flexible, grasping tail.
